Remembering Nestor

This tribute was created in memory of our loving friend, husband, father and grandfather.

Biography

A Celebration of Life

Nestor Nacario was born on July 10th in San Nicolas, Iriga, to Gregorio and Remedios Nacario, and was the second of seven siblings. After completing high school, Nestor attended UNEP, where he met and married Dolores.

Nestor was a dedicated member of the Alpha Omega fraternity. He also served his community as a former barangay tanod. Throughout his life, he took pride in his role within the community.

Nestor's passions included playing billiards, and, of course, drinking with friends. His devotion was clear in the way he always made time to enjoy life.

Nestor is survived by his sister, Nene; his brother, Fabian; his two sons, Junior and Sisa, and their spouses; as well as his two grandkids: Basilio and Crispin.
